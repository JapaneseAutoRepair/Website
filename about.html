About Japanese Auto Repair

Delivering expert care for Japanese vehicles with a commitment to quality and customer satisfaction since 2008.

Our Story

Founded in 2008, Japanese Auto Repair started with a simple mission: to provide top-notch repair and maintenance services tailored specifically for Japanese vehicles. Over the years, we’ve grown into a trusted name in Provo, UT, known for our expertise, reliability, and dedication to keeping your car on the road.


With decades of combined experience, our team uses the latest diagnostic tools and techniques to ensure every repair meets the highest standards. Whether it’s a routine oil change or a complex engine rebuild, we treat every vehicle with the care it deserves.
